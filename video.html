Sokcho Weekend [video diary]

Filmed on Cannon G7x Mark II, edited with Finalcut Pro X.

Sokcho is a seaside fishing town in South Korea. I remember eating Ritz crackers, sleping on tiny floor mattresses, and eating rice cakes outside of the temple.

New Years Eve [video diary]

Filmed on Cannon G7x Mark II, edited with Finalcut Pro X.

I don't like the cold, I don't like broomball, but I like it here. It's a real trick to hold a camera in -25°F (-33°C) weather. Whenever I hear Fleet Foxes, I always think about driving up the Gunflint Trail.

Rose Lake [video diary]

Filmed on Cannon G7x Mark II, edited on Finalcut Pro X.

A sweet (cold) afternoon on Rose Lake with my September COVID-19 pod, but more importantly, my first experience with neoprene paddling gloves. If I could be any tree in the Boundary Waters, I would probably be a pincherry.
