Functional Ceramics

Stoneware and Porcelain
[Cone 6]

In 2017, I spent a year in Northfield, Minnesota, learning the "ins and outs" of functional ceramics at St. Olaf College.

I spent over 25 weekly hours in the studio conducting glaze studies and throwing mugs (so... so many mugs). At the end of my stay, I had thrown over 500 functional pieces (though I kept just 30) and a full set of glaze studies for future studio reference.

Someday, I'll build a woodfire kiln in my back yard...
